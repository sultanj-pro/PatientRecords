# Patient Health Record Data Reference

This document provides a comprehensive list of data typically found in patient health records, following standard EHR (Electronic Health Record) and EMR (Electronic Medical Record) structures.

## **Demographics & Identification**
- Full name (legal, preferred)
- Date of birth
- Gender/sex assigned at birth
- Social Security Number
- Medical Record Number (MRN)
- Contact information (address, phone, email)
- Emergency contacts
- Preferred language
- Race/ethnicity
- Marital status
- Insurance information (primary, secondary, tertiary)

## **Vital Signs**
- Blood pressure (systolic/diastolic)
- Heart rate/pulse
- Respiratory rate
- Temperature
- Height
- Weight
- BMI (Body Mass Index)
- Oxygen saturation (SpO2)
- Pain level (1-10 scale)

## **Medical History**
- Past illnesses
- Chronic conditions/diagnoses
- Previous surgeries/procedures
- Hospitalizations
- Significant medical events
- Congenital conditions

## **Medications**
- Current medications (name, dosage, frequency, route, prescriber)
- Past medications
- Over-the-counter medications
- Supplements/vitamins
- Start/stop dates
- Refill history

## **Allergies & Adverse Reactions**
- Drug allergies
- Food allergies
- Environmental allergies
- Severity (mild, moderate, severe, life-threatening)
- Reaction type (rash, anaphylaxis, etc.)

## **Immunizations**
- Vaccine name
- Date administered
- Lot number
- Administrator
- Site of administration
- Adverse reactions

## **Laboratory Results**
- Complete Blood Count (CBC)
- Metabolic panels
- Lipid panels
- Urinalysis
- Cultures (blood, urine, wound)
- Pathology reports
- Tumor markers
- Hormone levels
- Genetic testing results

## **Diagnostic Imaging**
- X-rays
- CT scans
- MRI scans
- Ultrasounds
- PET scans
- Mammograms
- DEXA scans (bone density)
- Reports and findings

## **Procedures**
- Surgical procedures
- Diagnostic procedures
- Date performed
- Surgeon/provider
- Location/facility
- Complications
- Outcomes

## **Visits/Encounters**
- Office visits
- Emergency room visits
- Hospital admissions/discharges
- Telemedicine visits
- Date and time
- Provider seen
- Chief complaint
- Diagnosis codes (ICD-10)
- Procedure codes (CPT)

## **Problem List/Diagnoses**
- Active diagnoses
- Resolved diagnoses
- ICD-10 codes
- Date of onset
- Status (active, chronic, resolved)

## **Family History**
- Hereditary conditions
- Family member relationships
- Age of onset in relatives
- Causes of death in family

## **Social History**
- Smoking status (current, former, never)
- Alcohol use
- Drug use/substance abuse
- Occupation
- Living situation
- Exercise habits
- Diet
- Sexual history
- Education level

## **Functional Status**
- Activities of Daily Living (ADLs)
- Instrumental ADLs
- Mobility status
- Cognitive function
- Fall risk assessment

## **Mental Health**
- Psychiatric diagnoses
- Mental health assessments (PHQ-9, GAD-7)
- Counseling/therapy notes
- Mental health medications
- Suicide risk assessments

## **Care Plan**
- Treatment goals
- Care plan activities
- Care team members
- Patient education provided
- Follow-up instructions

## **Progress Notes**
- SOAP notes (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan)
- Provider documentation
- Nurse notes
- Specialist consultations
- Discharge summaries

## **Orders**
- Medication orders
- Laboratory orders
- Imaging orders
- Referrals to specialists
- Durable Medical Equipment (DME) orders
- Home health orders

## **Care Team**
- Primary Care Physician
- Specialists
- Nurses
- Care coordinators
- Pharmacists
- Therapists (PT, OT, speech)

## **Advanced Directives**
- Do Not Resuscitate (DNR)
- Living will
- Healthcare proxy/power of attorney
- POLST forms

## **OB/GYN History** (if applicable)
- Pregnancy history (G/P/A)
- Prenatal records
- Delivery records
- Contraception method
- PAP smear results
- Mammogram results

## **Pediatric-Specific**
- Growth charts
- Developmental milestones
- School records
- Newborn screening results

## **Administrative**
- Consent forms
- HIPAA authorization
- Patient portal access
- Communication preferences
- Advance beneficiary notices

## **Billing/Financial**
- Claims history
- Payment history
- Copays/deductibles
- Insurance authorizations

## **Quality Measures**
- Preventive care tracking (colonoscopy due, flu shot, etc.)
- Disease-specific quality metrics
- Patient satisfaction scores
